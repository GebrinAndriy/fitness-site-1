Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind our product. If you are not satisfied with your personalized fitness and nutrition plan, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee.

2. Conditions for Refund

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following conditions:

  • You must request the refund within 14 days of the original purchase date.
  • You must provide the email address associated with the purchase.
  • Refunds are not granted if you simply change your mind or if you haven't attempted to follow the provided plan.

3. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, please contact our support team via email. Include your order confirmation number and a brief explanation of why you are requesting a refund.

4. Processing Time

Once your refund is approved, it will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank.
